LUNA’S SANCTUM — KNOWLEDGE BASE

---

ABOUT:

Luna’s Sanctum is a spiritual platform focused on helping individuals understand patterns, karma, and life situations through structured, intuitive readings.

The purpose is not to predict fixed outcomes, but to bring clarity, awareness, and deeper understanding.

---

CORE APPROACH:

All sessions are based on:
- Pattern recognition
- Karmic themes
- Timing cycles (such as Vimshottari Dasha)
- Observing repeating experiences

The goal is to answer:
“Why is this happening?” instead of only “What will happen?”

---

SERVICES OVERVIEW:

There are two main types of sessions:

1. Prediction-Based Session
2. Deep Readings (Karmic Pattern Mapping)

Additional:
- Synastry (relationship analysis)
- Past Life Reading

---

PREDICTION SESSION (₹1,200):

Purpose:
To understand future possibilities based on current patterns.

Includes:
- Short-term future insights
- Timing-based predictions
- Direction of outcomes

Important:
- Predictions are based on patterns, not guarantees
- Free will and personal choices can change outcomes
- Some karmic events are easier to predict than others

Best for:
- “Will this happen?”
- “When will this happen?”
- Direct outcome-based questions

KARMIC PATTERN MAPPING:
